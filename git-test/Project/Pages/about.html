Welcome

SaLLy's photography Studio SpS was founded in 2012
by Miss Baba Salma and has been active from 2013 to present.
We began with a team of 4 and have now expanded with a total of 34 members presently.
We also expanded our services and deal with graphic designing as well.

Our main aim is to showcase the touchy meaning behind works of diffrent kind and
inspire the young to aspire for more. We host exhibitons, and have a gallery room to showcase all our beautiful artworks and designs.
We guarentee to capture those beautiful and special moments which you can hold on to forever.
We are located on the Spintex road, opposite the China Mall, Accra, Ghana.
One can also contact us by heading to Our Contact Us page
and give us a call or an email.
